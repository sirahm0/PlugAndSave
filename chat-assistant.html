Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI assistant work?

Our AI assistant uses your device data and energy usage patterns to provide personalized recommendations and answer your questions about energy consumption and savings.

What kind of questions can I ask?

You can ask about your energy usage, device efficiency, tips for saving energy, understanding your bills, and recommendations for optimizing your energy consumption.

Is my conversation data private?

Yes, your conversation data is encrypted and stored securely. We use it only to improve your experience and provide better recommendations.
